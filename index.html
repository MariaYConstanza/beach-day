About Us

As a group of aspiring software developers based out of Florida, we created this application to provide our users with the most up-to-date weather conditions to help our fellow surfers catch the biggest wave. Track tide activity, water levels and more to grab your board and plan your next "sick" day. If you would like to view more of our collaborators' work, please check out our GitHub profiles to view our past and/or ongoing projects below!
